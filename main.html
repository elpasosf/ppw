Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

What materials do you offer?

We offer a variety of high-quality materials including durable ABS like resin and various 4k resins.

How long does printing take?

Printing times vary based on size and complexity, typically ranging from a few hours to a couple of days. You'll get a precise estimate with your quote.

Can I upload my own designs?

Absolutely! We support STL, OBJ, and many other file types and even offer integration with Sketchfab for easy uploading of your 3D models.

What is the maximum print size?

Our large format printing can handle objects up to 165mm. For smaller, intricate designs, we support sizes down to 1mm.

How is pricing determined?

Pricing is calculated based on the dimensions of your print and the print's height varying from $20-$50.
